Works

WELLNESS-Mental Health Website

A mental health tracking platform that empowers users to monitor their emotional well-being through daily mood logs and psychological assessments. If a user consistently shows signs of distress for over a week, the system automatically offers the option to chat with a trained psychological intern for early intervention. The platform also includes scientifically-backed mental health tests and provides personalized recommendations, including therapist appointment suggestions when needed.



MaceBlog

A blogging platform exclusively for students of MACE to share their ideas, technical articles, experiences, and campus stories. The platform enables user authentication, blog creation, editing, and publishing — fostering a culture of writing and knowledge sharing within the college community.



MaceCalender

a centralized event management system for MACE where students can easily view upcoming events, their timings, and details in a clean calendar interface. Club leads and faculty members are given authenticated access to upload, update, and manage event entries, making coordination across the campus smooth and organized.



Protein Structure Prediction

A system using AlphaFold2 and ColabFold to accurately predict protein structures from amino acid sequences. Integrated 3D molecular visualization using interactive tools to explore spatial folding and structural features. Extended functionality to analyze protein-protein interactions through graph-based modeling and molecular docking techniques for research and pharmaceutical insights.
